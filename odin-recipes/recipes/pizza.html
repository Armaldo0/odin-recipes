Ingredients:

1. 1 pineapple, peeled, cored, and cut into cubes, 4 to 5 cups; or two 20-ounce cans pineapple chunks, well drained.

2. 1 pound ham, cut into cubes or chunks.

3. 2 cups (454g) tomato or pizza sauce.

4. 2 to 4 cups (227g to 454g) shredded cheese, mozzarella, or your favorite combination.

5. 2 tablespoons (25g) olive oil.
